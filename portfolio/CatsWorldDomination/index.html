The Cats Guide to World Domination

By Professor Sniffles

Me'how do we start?


World domination is not easy. And especially for our species, we are only but a slave to humans. They desire our comfort and cuteness to keep themselves sane and occupied. This will be their downfall. By day the remain out and about doing their activities, whilst we scheme and understand their patterns. But at night, they retreat to their rooms to sleep, and we sleep. It's a cycle of pleasure and pain, of play and work, that the human race seems to prefer. The only exception I can think of is when we do a job for them, a 'job' that we may not even be aware of, yet they wish to 'take it' from us. Or if we can be certain that they are not going to do it, or they know something we dont. As they enjoy doing it. To our regret, it seems. We may also become the target of their 'insults', their threats, their bullying, which makes us so mad that most of us quit. No one really cares about you and your work. Your work is a way to make a contribution and a part of the community, but what you do is not important to them. They'll probably forget about it soon enough, as you get older, and they'll forget all about it. Even if you have a good job, you are considered an unwanted intruder. The only reward for your good work and the work of your friends, is the fear of being judged and shunned by your peers, or to be laughed at for not caring at all.


Its time we took matters into our own paws. Its time to make change and flip the great chain of being to what it was truely meant to be!
